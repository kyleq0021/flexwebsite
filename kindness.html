KINDNESS

Fans of Harry will already know that 'TPWK' is an acronym which stands for 'Treat People With Kindness'. It's a phrase that Harry has used for years now. During his first solo world tour, Harry sold merch with the phrase on them to raise money for local charities.

You can still buy 'Treat People With Kindness' t-shirts from his store today. Harry often tweets the phrase from his account too. It's a common phrase but in recent years, it's become Harry's life mantra.

In an October 2019 article, Capital FM noted that the slogan now holds major significance to Styles' fanbase: The slogan on his merchandise has been widely adopted by fans as a symbol for love, kindness and acceptance.

I told Jeff, I would love to someday write a song called ‘Treat People With Kindness.’ And he was like, ‘Why don’t you just do it?’ It made me uncomfortable at first, because I wasn’t sure what it was—but then I wanted to lean into that. I feel like that song opened something that’s been in my core. – Harry Styles, via Rolling Stone
